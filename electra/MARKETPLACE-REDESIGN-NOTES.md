# Marketplace Redesign - Real Marketplace Look

## New Features:
1. **Seller Information**: Name, location, verification badge
2. **Dual Currency**: SUI and USDC payment options
3. **Payment Modal**: Choose payment method before purchase
4. **Product Cards**: Professional e-commerce style
5. **Price Display**: Per Wh and total price
6. **Trust Indicators**: Verified badges, trust scores
7. **Location Tags**: Geographic information
8. **Responsive Grid**: 1-3 columns based on screen size

## Payment Flow:
1. Click "Buy Now" on listing
2. Payment modal opens
3. Choose SUI or USDC
4. Confirm purchase
5. Show receipt

## Card Design:
- Seller name and verification badge
- Location with map pin icon
- Energy amount with lightning icon
- Price per Wh
- Total price in SUI and USDC
- Trust score with shield icon
- Buy button with payment options

## Payment Modal:
- Listing summary
- Payment method selector (SUI/USDC)
- Price display in selected currency
- Confirm button
- Cancel button
- Animated transitions
